# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want this project to be welcoming, direct, and professional.

Examples of positive behavior:

- assuming good intent
- giving actionable review feedback
- discussing code, not people
- documenting tradeoffs clearly
- being respectful across experience levels and platforms

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- doxxing or threats
- intentionally hostile review behavior
- derailing technical discussion with abuse

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block participation for behavior that makes collaboration unsafe or unproductive.

## Reporting

If needed, contact the maintainers listed in `MAINTAINERS.md`.
